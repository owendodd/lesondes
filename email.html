It's with great excitement that we are announcing a very special project co-organized by La Bleue. Les Ondes is a small three-day gathering focused on music, wine and food in the coastal town of Cerbère from May 29–31, 2026.

Set in the iconic art deco Hôtel Belvédère du Rayon Vert, the weekend will be an intimate opportunity for concentrated listening, sharing meals and wine, and spending time next to the ocean during the first moments of summer.

The music program will take place from Friday afternoon to Sunday afternoon, with performances taking place in multiple spaces throughout the hotel and in the surrounding area. The featured artists include: Miriam Adefris, Pierre Bastien, CTM, Lukas de Clerck, Maya Dhondt, Mats Erlandsson, Elisabeth Klinck, Louis Laurain, Chantal Michelle, Mohammad Reza Mortazavi, Fredrik Rasten, and Youmna Saba, with some additional surprises to be announced.

Communal meals will be prepared by Harry Lester and his team from Comptoir Central des Bazars in Clermont Ferrand and a carefully selected wine program is being organized by Clara Blum. Meals start with dinner together Friday evening, lunch and dinner on Saturday, and finish with a group lunch on Sunday, and our bar will be accessible throughout the weekend.

Capacity is limited to 150 people. For information and tickets, visit les-ondes.fr.

Thanks so much and hope to see you there! Let us know if you have any questions.
